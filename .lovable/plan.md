
# Taekwondo Team Website

A clean, modern informational website to showcase your taekwondo team, with a news feed, class schedules, team profiles, and a contact form.

## Pages & Structure

### 1. Sticky Header / Navigation Bar
- Small logo + team name pinned at the top
- Navigation links to all pages
- Mobile-friendly hamburger menu

### 2. Home Page
- Hero section with a striking image/banner and team tagline
- **News Feed** section — a scrollable list of announcements (upcoming competitions, belt promotions, events) managed as simple data in the code
- Quick links to classes and contact

### 3. About / Intro Page
- Team history, mission, and philosophy
- What is Taekwondo — brief intro for newcomers
- Why join the team

### 4. Schedule & Classes
- Weekly timetable displayed in a clean table/grid format
- Class descriptions (kids, beginners, advanced, competition team)
- Training location and hours

### 5. Team & Coaches
- Coach profiles with photo, bio, and belt rank
- Optional: team member highlights or competition roster

### 6. Gallery & Achievements
- Photo gallery showcasing training, events, and competitions
- Achievements section: medals, competition results, belt promotions

### 7. Contact Page
- Contact form (name, email, message) with validation
- Location map or address info
- Social media links
- Training hours summary

## Design Style
- **Clean & modern** aesthetic
- White/light backgrounds with subtle accent colors (e.g., navy blue or red accents for martial arts energy)
- Clear typography, generous spacing
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop

## Technical Approach
- Fully static, no backend or login needed
- Contact form will show a success message (can be connected to an email service later)
- News items and schedules stored as simple data arrays in code — easy to update
- Placeholder images that you can replace with your own team photos
